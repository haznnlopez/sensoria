The Cluster · Why It Matters

Why Biomechanical Sensing
Belongs on Your Body

The Cluster is more than a collection of wearable technologies — it is a framework for understanding how the human body interacts with data. By mapping movement, posture, and strain across the wrist, head, and torso, it turns invisible physical patterns into measurable insights that can prevent injury, improve performance, and reshape how we design human-centered technology.
