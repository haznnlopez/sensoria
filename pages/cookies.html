Cookie Policy

On this page

Short version: sensoria uses only essential cookies to make the site work. No tracking. No advertising. No analytics that identify you. That's it.

01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember information about your visit — like whether a menu was open, or which language you prefer.

Cookies cannot run programs or carry viruses. They are just text. Most websites use some form of cookies to function properly.

02

What Cookies We Use

sensoria is a static website. The only cookies present are essential ones required for the site to function and for the Bootstrap UI framework to operate correctly.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
bs_navbar Remembers whether the mobile navigation menu is open or closed Session Essential
sensoria_pref Stores any user display preferences if set (e.g. reduced motion) 30 days Essential

Session cookies are deleted automatically when you close your browser. They are never stored permanently on your device.

03

What We Do NOT Use

We want to be clear about what is absent from sensoria:

  • No analytics cookies — we do not use Google Analytics, Hotjar, Mixpanel, or any equivalent service.
  • No advertising cookies — we run no ads. No ad network cookies are present.
  • No social tracking pixels — no Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or similar tracking tools.
  • No cross-site tracking — we do not track you across other websites.
  • No profiling or fingerprinting — we make no attempt to build a profile of your browsing behaviour.

sensoria has zero commercial interest in your data. We are a student project. The only thing we want you to do is read and learn.

04

Third-Party Cookies

Some external services that power parts of sensoria may set their own cookies. These are limited to:

  • Google Fonts — may set a short-lived cookie to serve font files efficiently. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • jsDelivr CDN — may set performance cookies when loading Bootstrap and icon libraries. Their privacy policy applies.

Neither of these services uses cookies to track you for advertising purposes, and neither receives any personal data from you via sensoria.

05

Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Below are quick links to cookie management pages for major browsers:

Browser Cookie Settings
Chrome — Manage cookies in Chrome
Firefox — Manage cookies in Firefox
Safari — Manage cookies in Safari
Edge — Manage cookies in Edge

Disabling cookies may prevent minor UI features from functioning (like the mobile menu state), but will not block you from reading any content on sensoria.

06

Changes to This Policy

If our cookie use changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date at the top. As a student project, significant changes are unlikely before our submission deadline.
