The argument

Your body shows damage before you feel it.

Most injuries are not sudden—they are the result of thousands of unnoticed micro-movements.

The modern lifestyle places unnatural stress on the body through repetition, poor posture, and prolonged inactivity.

The Cluster changes this by placing sensors at key points—wrist, head, and torso—where movement and load are most critical.

These sensors track motion continuously, detecting inefficiencies and risks long before pain appears.

This allows users to correct behavior early, making prevention part of daily life—not something that happens after injury.
