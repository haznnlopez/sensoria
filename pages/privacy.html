Privacy Policy

On this page

Effective April 12, 2026

sensoria is a student educational project by The Ergo Lab (Group 4, CIP 1101-CSAD). We don't sell your data, run ads, or store anything we don't need. This policy explains the very little we do collect — and why.

01

Who We Are

sensoria is an educational website created by The Ergo Lab, a student group from CIP 1101-CSAD at our institution in Manila, Philippines. The site is a course project exploring the intersection of wearable technology and human ergonomics.

For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at theergolab@sensoria.com or through our Instagram @_theergolab.

02

What We Collect

Because sensoria is a static educational website, we collect very little. Here is a full account of what may be gathered when you visit:

  • Contact form submissions — if you use our contact form on the About page, we receive your name, email address, and message content.
  • Newsletter sign-ups — if you subscribe via our newsletter form, we collect your email address only.
  • Automatically collected data — like all websites, our hosting provider may log your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited. We do not access or use this data for tracking purposes.

We do not require account creation, do not collect payment information, and do not ask for any sensitive personal data.

03

How We Use It

Any information you voluntarily provide (contact form, newsletter) is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or message
  • Send you new article updates if you subscribed
  • Improve the site based on general feedback

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties. Full stop.

04

Cookies

sensoria uses only essential cookies — small files placed in your browser that help the site function correctly. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies that identify you personally.

For a full breakdown of what cookies are used and why, see our Cookie Policy.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. This may affect minor site functionality but will not prevent you from reading any content on sensoria.

05

Third Parties

sensoria loads some resources from trusted third-party CDNs to ensure fast performance:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Playfair Display, Montserrat). Google may log your IP as part of this request.
  • Bootstrap CDN (jsDelivr) — for layout and UI components.
  • Bootstrap Icons CDN — for iconography.

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal data with any of them, and none of them receive your contact form or newsletter data.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other behavioral tracking services.

06

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter by replying to any email or contacting us directly
  • Withdraw consent for any processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at theergolab@sensoria.com. We will respond within 7 days.

07

Data Retention

Contact form messages are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Newsletter email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe. We do not retain any other personal data.

08

Security

sensoria is a static website hosted on a standard web host. While we take reasonable precautions, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please do not send sensitive personal information through our contact form.

09

Children

sensoria is an educational site suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data through our site, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. As a student project with a limited lifespan, significant changes are unlikely. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11

Contact

Privacy questions, data requests, or concerns can be directed to:

  • Email: theergolab@sensoria.com
  • Instagram: @_theergolab
  • Facebook: The Ergo Lab
  • Location: Manila, Philippines
